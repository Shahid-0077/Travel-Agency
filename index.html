Travel Tips for India

  • Best time to visit: Oct–Mar for most regions.
  • Carry digital & physical copies of your IDs.
  • Use UPI for fast and safe payments.
  • Try local cuisine but stay hydrated.
  • Respect cultural sites and dress modestly when required.
